About

For the past several years I've been the sole developer at Koinonia Family Services, building and maintaining Django applications on Google Cloud that help social workers support foster families. I'm an old-school programmer who can still debug without AI, yet I pioneered the use of tools like Microsoft Copilot and Claude Code at Koinonia, using them mindfully and with great care. Before Koinonia I was a senior engineer at Lull and a lead instructor at Galvanize's software engineering bootcamp, where I taught eight cohorts.
